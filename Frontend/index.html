About Me

Full-Stack Developer & Business Solutions Expert

My name is Victor Moses Mwakyoma, a Computer Science major at the University of Dar es Salaam (UDSM) in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. I started with a strong science foundation at Mzumbe Secondary School, where I studied the PCM combination (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics), and turned that into solving real business problems with software.

Over the last few years I’ve delivered 15+ real-world projects for offices, schools, shops, and supermarkets. Through customer relations training under StartHub, I’ve learned how to listen to non-technical clients, understand their challenges, and turn them into clear technical solutions.

Today I work as a full-stack developer focused on HR systems, eCommerce platforms, School Management systems, POS systems, and booking platforms for Tanzanian businesses — building dashboards and tools that make work easier, faster, and more accurate.
